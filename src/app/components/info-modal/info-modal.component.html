About

Petal Timer is a cute and motivating Pomodoro timer designed for real 9-5 workdays. Customize your work hours, break durations, and theme. Stay focused, earn badges, and get things done.

Privacy

This app does not collect or send any personal data. Your settings are saved in your browser only via localStorage.
